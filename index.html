About

The Assignment Discord Bot connects your Canvas courses to your Discord server. It syncs assignments, posts a weekly Monday summary, and sends gentle reminders for planned study sessions and upcoming due dates. Built with Python, SQLite, and the Canvas API, it's designed to be simple to run and helpful for students and study groups.
